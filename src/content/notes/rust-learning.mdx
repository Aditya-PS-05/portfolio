## Ownership Rules

1. Each value has an owner
2. Only one owner at a time
3. Value dropped when owner goes out of scope

## Borrowing

- Immutable references: `&T`
- Mutable references: `&mut T`
- Can't have mutable and immutable refs simultaneously
